About Me

Eric MacDonald is a 1991 graduate of the University of New Hampshire, majoring in Electrical Engineering. His early career focused on hardware development of compuer networking equipment. Later, he diversified his time to include private equity investments in real estate, technology and retail franchises.

Eric has held board positions with several non-profits, including th American Indepenence Museum, Family Promise, and the Executive Advisory Board at the InterOperability Lab at the University of New Hampshire.

Eric splits his time between Exeter NH where he and his wife raised their three daughters, and Sebago Maine, where he often spends time breaking the propeller off his boat.
